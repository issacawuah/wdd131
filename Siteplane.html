Project Site Plan

Shopping Cart Web Application

My Awesome Shopping Site

This name clearly indicates the purpose of the site, which is to provide a platform for users to manage their shopping activities.

shoppingcutapp.com

Site Purpose

The Shopping Cart Web Application enables users to add, remove, and view items in a virtual shopping cart. Users can input item names and track their total spending through an intuitive interface.

Scenarios

  • How can I add items to my shopping cart?
  • How do I remove an item from my shopping cart?
  • What is the total cost of the items in my cart?
